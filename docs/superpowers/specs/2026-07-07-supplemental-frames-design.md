# Supplemental Video Frames — Design

## Goal

Supplement the existing 13 approved hero frames (cover + Scene 1–13) with additional
evidentiary and illustrative frames, so long scenes do not sit too long on a single image
and every major claim is backed by an on-screen primary-source receipt.

The narrated 13-scene structure, timing, and narration in
`docs/superpowers/specs/2026-07-04-video-script.md` do **not** change. This spec only adds
B-roll / cutaway frames placed *within* existing scenes.

## Non-negotiable principles

1. **Keep every hero.** `cover page.png` and `Scene 1.jpg … scene 13.png` in
   `Screenshots Video/` are the approved heroes and are never replaced or edited. All new
   frames are inserts shown between hero holds, or replacements only for the crude
   placeholder motifs in the generator's `generated/` tier.
2. **Source-discipline.** Every evidentiary frame is either (a) a real PDF/photo screenshot,
   or (b) a verbatim quote of an official reply with a citation line (sender · date · subject).
   No author paraphrase presented as an official statement.
3. **Match the approved slide template.** Evidentiary frames use the existing
   `render_pdf_source()` / `image_frame()` / `save_card()` styling in
   `scripts/generate_supplemental_frames.py`: white/navy title bar, red rule, highlighted
   clause, footer caption, subtitle-safe bottom band.
4. **Targeted density.** Roughly 1–2 inserts per long scene. ~16 new frames total.
