SUMMARY OF FACTS 1. On 1 July 2022, the PDPC was informed by the Organisation about the
deployment of ransomware on its network. 2. As a result, the personal data of 18,135 individuals including their names,
personal contact information, identification numbers, employment records, date of birth, race, religion, COVID-19 test results and vaccination information, shareholding information, employee bank account number, profile photographs and fingerprints were encrypted and/or exfiltrated. 3. To prevent a recurrence of a similar incident, the Organisation took immediate remedial action to address the cause of the personal data breach.
