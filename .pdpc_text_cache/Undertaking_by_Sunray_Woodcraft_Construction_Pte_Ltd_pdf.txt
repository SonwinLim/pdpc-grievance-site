SUMMARY OF FACTS 1. On 11 May 2023, the PDPC was notified by Sunray Woodcraft Construction
Pte Ltd SWCPL of a ransomware attack on its servers on or about 25 April 2023 causing loss of access to IT systems and encryption of files with personal data. 2. As a result, the personal data of 2,130 individuals including their names, addresses, personal email addresses (in respect of 689 individuals), telephone numbers, NRIC numbers, passport numbers, photographs, dates of birth, bank account numbers and salary information was affected. 3. To prevent a recurrence of a similar incident, the Organisation took immediate remedial action to address the cause of the personal data breach.
